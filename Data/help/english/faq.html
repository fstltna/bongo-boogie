Frequently Asked Questions

The game does not start !?

Check that you have at least 8 MB of video memory and that the drivers are properly installed.
The game does not run on S3 Savage 4 video card ( they do not provide directdraw acceleration )

Can I change the password for the parental lock ?

Yes, you can edit it in the plock.txt file that is located into the game folder, replace
parent
by
yourpassword

How do I reset the scores ?

Go in the options panel and click on reset scores.

The game is too slow when I play in a window !

Try reducing the depth of your screen in 16Bits per pixel, if it is still too slow, try to play fullscreen, this will drastically improve performance.
If it is still too slow, consider upgrading your video card.

I lost my Registration Code !

Just send us an email with your contact information, we will be happy to send it back to you.
