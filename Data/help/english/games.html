Doulber is a friendly ant, though a little greedy. His favorite dessert? Diamonds! He finds them as he burrows through the earth, but he has to be careful or a tunnel might collapse on him.

Doulber must also watch out for other dangers, including lava flows and a group of malicious creatures that includes his distant cousin, Darker.
