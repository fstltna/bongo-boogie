The welcome dialog box is the first window that the game will display.

Pressing HELP will take you to this HTML help file.

The Fullscreen checkbox will let you play in full screen.
Try playing in fullscreen if you experience slow gaming when you play in a window.

The Parental Lock check box will enable/disable agressive content.

The Play button will launch the game.

Quit will immediately quit this dialog box and return to the operating system.

By clicking in the languages list, you can select the language in which all the information are displayed.
