Guide the monkey through all the levels of BongoBoogie !

To continue his trip, you have to manouver the monkey and catch the balls that are falling,

in order to break all the boxes and packages that are on his way ! but... you never know what's in these boxes...

Controls :

Move the Monkey with the mouse, pushing left will move the monkey to the left, pushing it right will move it to the right.

Catch the bonuses ( not always...? ) that are falling out of the boxes in order to improve the monkey equipment.

You also press the mouse button to shoot at the bricks ( if you have the right bat in hand... )

Press the ESC key to quit the game.

Press 'P' to pause the game, pressing 'P' again will resume the game.
